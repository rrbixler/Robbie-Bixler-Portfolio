About Me

My background is in Architectural and Product Design. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Architecture from the University of San Diego, and have recently changed my career focus to Software Engineering. I have a passion for helping people to design in order to express themselves whether it be the home they want to live in, the product they want to build, or the web applications they want to create. Attention to detail is crucial in design and development, and that is my priority when it comes to software engineering. Delivering the intended product is never easy, however striving for perfection is what motivates me and drives my work ethic.
